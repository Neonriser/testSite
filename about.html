About Me

I've always wanted to do some HTML since I was small.

When I was a little child, I browsed around webpages in my free time. As I looked at the petpages on Neopets and the various websites in the 2000s, I thought to myself "Wow, that's so cool, I want to make a page like that."

And here I am now, making an HTML webpage as I type this, to help practice for that HTML test. :P

Not only I would thank Neocities for practicing HTML, but also Georgian College for helping me use that skill in an actual job.
