Team-416

Team-416 is a superhero group located in Toronto, Canada. Their goal is to protect their city and its outskirts from various kinds of harm.

Its members include but not limited to:

  • Wolfman
  • Danella Vera
  • Red Lightning
  • Qiana Ukuduma
  • Gizmo
  • Oprah
  • Jet Smoke
  • Zeta
  • Flaresight

The superhero team is led by Mr.Mend, a small man with psychic powers.
